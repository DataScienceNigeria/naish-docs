The Projects Archive gathers **Gates-funded and related development work** that
informs NAISH – the prior deployments, platforms, and delivery partners whose
experience shapes the Hub's identified use cases. NAISH is not starting from
zero: many of its target use cases (agricultural advisory, digital health
coaching, health-record digitisation, frontline data collection) have direct
antecedents in programmes that have already run at national scale across Nigeria
and the wider Global South.

This section documents those projects so a builder, partner, or reviewer can see
the lineage behind each use case and reuse the platforms, datasets, and lessons
that already exist.

## Why this archive matters

- **Reuse over reinvention** – several NAISH use cases can adopt or extend
  existing platforms rather than build new ones.
- **Evidence base** – prior deployments carry peer-reviewed evidence of impact,
  which strengthens the case for the Hub.
- **Delivery partners** – the organisations behind these projects are candidate
  implementers for NAISH use cases.
