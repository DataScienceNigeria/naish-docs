**CommCare** is a no-code platform for building mobile and web data-collection
and program-delivery applications, built by **Dimagi**. It lets teams create
production-ready apps for frontline workers without writing code, and it is one
of the most widely deployed digital-health and development platforms in the
Global South. It is included in this archive because several NAISH use cases – digital health coaching, health-record digitisation, and frontline service
delivery – map directly onto what CommCare already does at scale.

Reference: [dimagi.com/commcare](https://dimagi.com/commcare/)

## What CommCare is

CommCare is a **no-code application builder** aimed at frontline teams. Users
build custom apps for data collection and case management without any coding
experience, then deploy them to community health workers, agricultural
extension agents, field researchers, and other frontline staff.

Its defining characteristics:

- **Offline-first** – apps work without an internet connection and sync when
  connectivity returns, which is essential for rural and low-connectivity
  deployments.
- **Case management** – tracks individuals over time (a patient, a household, a
  farm), not just one-off form submissions.
- **Real-time reporting** – dashboards and reports for program managers.

## About Dimagi

Dimagi is the organisation behind CommCare. It provides integrated data
collection and program-delivery solutions for **global health and development
work**, positioning CommCare as the delivery layer between program design and
frontline execution.

## Key features

- Offline-first data collection (works without internet)
- Advanced case management for tracking individuals over time
- Real-time reporting and dashboards
- Compliance: **HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2**
- Security: two-factor authentication and role-based permissions

## Relevance to NAISH

CommCare is a proven delivery layer for exactly the kind of frontline,
offline-first, case-management workloads several NAISH use cases require:

- **Self-CAIRE digital health coaching** – CommCare-style case management and
  frontline delivery align with tracking members and health interactions over
  time.
- **Health-record digitisation** – structured data capture and case management
  complement the OCR / NLP pipeline that converts paper records into DHIS2 / EHR
  formats.
- **Agricultural advisory** – frontline extension-worker apps are a native
  CommCare pattern.

Rather than the Hub building bespoke frontline apps, use-case teams can consider
adopting or integrating an existing, evidence-backed platform like CommCare and
focusing NAISH GPU capacity on the AI/model layer.
