**Dimagi** is a US-based social enterprise (founded 2002, headquartered in
Cambridge, Massachusetts, with offices in Washington DC, New Delhi, and Cape
Town) that builds **open-source digital tools for frontline health and
development work** in low-resource settings. It is a certified B Corporation and
a public-benefit corporation, and one of the longest-standing technology
partners in global health. It is included in this archive because its
platforms – CommCare, Open Chat Studio, CommCare Connect, and SureAdhere – are
proven, evidence-backed delivery layers for exactly the frontline, offline-first,
multilingual workloads several NAISH use cases require.

Reference: [dimagi.com](https://dimagi.com/)

<Callout type="info">
  **Gates Foundation partner.** Dimagi received transformational core funding

  Gates *Grand Challenges: Catalyzing Equitable AI* grant to build an
  LLM-powered coach for frontline workers on top of Open Chat Studio (pilot in

## CommCare

**CommCare** is Dimagi's flagship **open-source, no-code platform** for building
mobile and web apps for frontline data collection, case management, and program
delivery. It is designed **offline-first** for challenging, low-connectivity
environments: frontline workers collect data, manage cases, and access job aids
without internet, syncing automatically when a connection returns.

Its defining characteristics:

- **Offline-first** – apps work without connectivity and sync on return,
  essential for rural and low-connectivity deployments.
- **Longitudinal case management** – tracks individuals, households, or entities
  over time and links cases across programs and service points (not just one-off
  form submissions).
- **Real-time reporting** – dashboards and reports for program managers.
- **Compliance & security** – HIPAA, GDPR, and SOC 2; two-factor auth and
  role-based permissions.

## Open Chat Studio

**Open Chat Studio (OCS)** is Dimagi's **open-source platform for building,
evaluating, and deploying LLM-powered chatbots** – positioned as "the
responsible layer between AI and the people it serves." It targets global-health
and social-impact organisations working in low-resource, low-literacy,
multilingual, and infrastructure-constrained settings.

Core capabilities:

- **Multi-LLM** – OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, Azure, Mistral, LLaMA, Groq,
  and more.
- **Multi-channel deployment** – WhatsApp, web widget, SMS, Telegram, Messenger,
  Slack, voice, email, and **CommCare Connect**.
- **Evaluation tooling** – systematic testing of bot behaviour before it reaches
  vulnerable users.
- **RAG / knowledge bases** – ground responses in verified local content.

Open source under a **BSD-3-Clause** license (Django/Celery/PostgreSQL+pgvector
backend, React/TypeScript frontend).

## Other platforms

### CommCare Connect

A **"pay-for-impact" service-delivery marketplace**: frontline workers learn
skills, deliver *verified* services, and earn income tied to completed
interventions rather than planned activity, with transparent payments to local
organisations and workers. Dimagi reports **1.5M+ services delivered**; a Uganda
pilot enrolled **675+ newborns**, ran **1,400 home visits**, and disbursed
**$20,000+** to workers.

### SureAdhere

A **virtual care platform for medication adherence** – video Directly Observed
Therapy for tuberculosis and other infectious diseases. Dimagi reports **96% TB
treatment completion** and a body of publications that have informed **CDC and
WHO TB treatment guidelines**.

### Gates Grand Challenges – LLM coach for frontline workers

Dimagi's flagship AI initiative (2023): *"Closing the Supervision Gap: An
LLM-Powered Coach for Frontline Workers,"* built on Open Chat Studio and CommCare
infrastructure, piloted in Malawi – one of ~50 Gates *Catalyzing Equitable AI*
grants.

- Announcement: [dimagi.com/grand-challenges-catalyzing-equitable-ai](https://dimagi.com/grand-challenges-catalyzing-equitable-ai/)

## Relevance to NAISH

Dimagi's stack is a proven delivery layer for the frontline, offline-first,
multilingual, case-management workloads several NAISH use cases require:

- **Self-CAIRE digital health coaching** – CommCare case management + an Open
  Chat Studio LLM assistant map directly onto tracking members and health
  interactions over time.
- **Health-record digitisation** – CommCare's structured capture and case
  management complement an OCR/NLP pipeline converting paper records into
  DHIS2/EHR formats.
- **Agricultural advisory** – frontline extension-worker apps are a native
  CommCare pattern; OCS adds a multilingual conversational layer.

Rather than build bespoke frontline apps and chatbots, use-case teams can adopt
or integrate Dimagi's evidence-backed, open-source platforms and focus NAISH GPU
capacity on the AI/model layer – with a shared Gates Foundation lineage easing
partnership.
